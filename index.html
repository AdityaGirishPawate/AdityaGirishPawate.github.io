About Me

I am Aditya Girish Pawate, a fourth-year undergraduate student majoring in Mathematics and Computing at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur Department of Mathematics which is among the elite 5 universities in India. I love playing Basketball and Table Tennis.

I always love to explore more fields of expertise. I have explored Artificial Intelligence, Web Design and Development, Financial Markets and Trading, Competitive Programming, Software Development and many more. As an undergraduate who has always been deeply fascinated by the applications of mathematics and computer science, I find my research interest gravitating towards the study of Artificial Intelligence. While this might be a very broad field I am particularly oriented towards Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, AI Planning, and Reinforcement Learning. I have explored this subject in-depth and I would like to utilize it effectively to address more real-world challenges. I believe that I am passionate, intellectually capable, and prepared to set out on this exhilarating and challenging path. Owing to my innate desire to further my knowledge and to innovate and upgrade my skills, I decided to embark upon a voyage of boundless possibilities. I believe that keeping an explorative attitude and inquisitive mind is the key to a constant learning process. As an undergraduate in applied mathematics in computer science, I have learned the basic principles of engineering and this has intrigued me to explore a wide range of opportunities for my future. I am also very interested in Financial Markets and entrepreneurship. This interest found expression when I founded a company for providing services in field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. I learned a lot of valuable lessons by founding the company from scratch. Some of the important lessons which helped me grow the business are quality assurance, team management and gaurenteed delivery of services.
