About Me

I am Aditya Girish Pawate, a undergraduate majoring in Mathematics and Computing at the Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur Department of Mathematics which is among the elite 5 universities in India. I have lived in Bangalore for the past 20 years. My hobbies include playing Basketball and Table Tennis.

Throughout my undergraduate studies, I have explored diverse fields of expertise such as Artificial Intelligence, Web Design and Development, Financial Markets and Trading, Competitive Programming, and Software Development. However, my fascination for the applications of mathematics and computer science has led me towards the study of Artificial Intelligence, particularly in the domains of Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, AI Planning, and Reinforcement Learning. As I delved deeper into the potential of AI, I realized the impact it can have on the healthcare sector. The healthcare industry is plagued by a host of challenges, such as long wait times, high costs, and limited access to care, which can be addressed through innovative AI solutions. Therefore, my goal is to become an AI entrepreneur in the healthcare sector, to utilize my skills and knowledge to provide efficient and affordable healthcare solutions to people in need. I am passionate, intellectually capable, and prepared to take on this challenging yet exhilarating path. I believe that keeping an explorative attitude and an inquisitive mind is the key to a constant learning process. My background in applied mathematics and computer science has provided me with a strong foundation in engineering principles, which I aim to leverage to explore a wide range of opportunities for my future. As an AI entrepreneur in the healthcare sector, I am committed to developing innovative AI-based solutions that can enhance patient care, optimize healthcare operations, and improve healthcare outcomes. I am driven by a deep sense of purpose to make a positive impact on society and to address critical healthcare challenges through the power of AI.
